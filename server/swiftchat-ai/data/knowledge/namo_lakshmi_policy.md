# Namo Lakshmi Yojana — Policy

## Scheme summary
₹50,000 disbursement over four academic years (Class 9 → Class 12) for girl students enrolled in government-recognised schools across Gujarat. The scheme is fully DBT — funds go directly to the mother's bank account.

## Eligibility
- Student is **female** and enrolled in **Class 9, 10, 11, or 12** of an eligible school.
- Family annual income **≤ ₹6,00,000**. Income above this auto-rejects the application.
- Student has a valid Aadhaar.
- Mother (or guardian, if mother is not available) has:
  - A valid Aadhaar number
  - An active bank account
  - **Mother's name on Aadhaar must match the name on the bank passbook exactly.** Mismatches are the #1 rejection reason.

## Required documents
- Student Aadhaar (front and back)
- Mother's Aadhaar
- Mother's bank passbook OR cancelled cheque
- Family income certificate (only when income field needs proof; below threshold is self-declared)
- Leaving Certificate / Birth Certificate (LCR)
- Student photograph

## Disbursement schedule
Monthly cycles are generated through the academic year. Each month the student's attendance is checked: ≥ 80% → cycle eligible; < 80% → manual approver review (the first month after enrolment is exempt).

## Common rejection reasons
- **Mother name mismatch** — Aadhaar spelling vs bank spelling differ.
- **Blurry Aadhaar image** — re-upload a clearer scan.
- **Income certificate missing** when proof is requested.
- **Bank details invalid** — wrong IFSC, account closed.
- **LCR missing** for new admissions.

All rejections can be corrected by the teacher via "Correct rejected" and resubmitted for CRC re-review.

## Opt-out
A guardian can decline the scholarship at any time. The teacher uploads a signed declaration PDF; the application moves to **Not wanted**. It can be reopened later.
