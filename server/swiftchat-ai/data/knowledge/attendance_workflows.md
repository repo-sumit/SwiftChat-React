# Attendance Workflows

## Who can mark attendance
Only **Teacher** and **Principal** roles can mark attendance. Parents see their own child's attendance only and cannot mark on behalf of others.

## Daily flow (Teacher)
1. From the home screen tap **Mark Attendance** or type "attendance" / "हाजरी" / "hajri" in chat.
2. Pick the class (Class 6, 7, 8, or 9). The roster opens inside the chat thread or right-side canvas with each student's name and roll number.
3. Tap each student to toggle Present / Absent. Bulk mark all present is also supported.
4. Tap **Submit Attendance**.
5. Parent SMS / WhatsApp alerts for absent students are queued automatically and sent at 5:00 PM unless cancelled.

## Editing past attendance
Past-date attendance is editable for the same day only. After 24 hours edits require Principal approval and an audit-logged reason.

## Below-threshold reporting
Students with attendance < 80% appear in the at-risk list and impact DigiVritti monthly payment cycles (see `pfms_payment_process.md`). The first month after enrolment is excluded from the threshold check.

## Multilingual phrasing the chat understands
- "Attendance mark karni hai"
- "Class 7 ni hajri lo"
- "Class 6 attendance kholo"
- "Who was absent today?"
- "Absent students dikhao"

## Reports
After submission, the Class Dashboard refreshes with today's attendance. The Principal's School Dashboard aggregates all classes. The DEO can drill down by block.
